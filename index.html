Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

How does the extension detect bias?

Our model is trained on over a million peer-reviewed articles and uses advanced NLP techniques to identify linguistic patterns associated with various types of bias.

Does the extension store my browsing data?

No. We never store or share your personal browsing data.

Will it slow down my browser?

Not at all. The extension is lightweight and optimized to run quickly in the background without affecting performance.

Is it free to use?

Yes! We offer a free tier with 10 analyses per week. Coming soon is our premium plan with higher access and advanced features.
